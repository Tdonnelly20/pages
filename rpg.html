Project Description

 Since September 2021, I have been a Senior Programmer for a Unity-based combat role-playing game that I've been working on with a team of other WPI students. Our RPG will be a full-length, professional-feeling, narrative RPG with action and combat components, complete with professional art, puzzles, and an original soundtrack. We recently released a vertical slice at WPI's video game showcase, Alphafest, where it received positive reviews.

 I researched, designed, and implemented the RPG's stat system and overworld system with C#. Through this project, I am developing skills with Jira, Unity, working in large teams, leading others, and gaining practical experience with C# and game development. I'll release more information on my work with the RPG here as the game progresses.
