Into The Storm

Role: Sole Programmer & Co-Writer
Studio: Spaced Out Studios
Tools: Python, Ren'Py
Duration: August - September 2021
Team Size: 3

Genre: Visual Novel
Platform: Windows, Mac, Linux
Project Status: Prototype
Repository: GitHub

Game Description

 Into the Storm is a retro-sci-fi themed visual novel that follows the player, a pilot, as they escort a mysterious woman trying to flee a city called Dometellium due to intense political unrest. In this game, the player must make a series of timed choices that will influence what choices the player can make in the future and which of the three endings they receive.

Process

 Under the mentorship of Brian Moriarty, I collaborated with two other people to create a visual novel prototype called Into the Storm. I worked as the sole programmer and co-writer for Into the Storm. Additionally, I was also in charge of creating surveys and gathering data from playtesters. I programmed the game with a mix of Python and Ren'Py, and the code for Into the Storm is available on my GitHub.

Data

 Into the Storm was a big success, as 88.2% of play testers enjoyed the story and world-building. Additionally, by designing and implementing the game's timed choice system, I increased the game's replay value to 77% among playtesters.
